# Mengke Zhang

**PhD, Architecture and Sciences of the City · EPFL**

I study the political economy of regional development, periphery urbanization, and infrastructure-led urban transformation in China. I use ethnographic and qualitative methods to examine how state strategy, mega-events, and financialized development reshape territories, institutions, and everyday life. My PhD investigated the mechanisms of extended urbanization driven by the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics.
